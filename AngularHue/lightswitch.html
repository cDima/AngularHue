Lightswitch allows you to control digitally connected Philip Hue lightbulbs — straight from your browser.

Home automation was never faster. One press — turning the lights on or off. Dim the lights with a mouse swipe. Choose a mood that suites you best and have the home natural — changing lights as nature does, continuesly. Chose from 16k colors for all the lamps, individually or for the whole house.

  • Dynamic themes

    Including Romantic Red, Disco, Sunrise, on individual light bulbs.

  • 16k Colors

    Full-color spectrum, including 9 Kelvin notes, 6 Fluorescent lights.

  • Simple & Minimal

    Auto bridge discovery. One-step setup. With a single tap - control.

  • Groups, Dimmer

    Control individual light bulbs, as well as groups. Single-click dimmer.

Lightswitch works as a plugin Google Chrome extension for Mac, Windows, and Chromebooks.
